# Shelf Swing

Shelf Swing teaches front swing with three focus charts arranged diagonally from front-left to back-right. The 2D Geometry panel uses a Top view so the subject trace and plane of sharp focus can be compared directly.

The physical calibration remains approximately `-3.802040434°` front swing and `3411.619 mm` focus distance. The public controls expose `0.1°` Swing steps and `10 mm` Focus steps, so the guided, keyboard-selectable solution is `-3.8°`, `3410 mm`, and f/11 or f/22, with Rise and Tilt at zero.

Shelf Swing is available at:

- `/simulator/free/shelf-swing`
- `/simulator/guided/shelf-swing/swing-01`

Both the 3D subject and Ground Glass use the registered Shelf Swing subject. Ground Glass is rendered through the RTT pipeline; the former Shelf-specific legacy HTML/CSS mockup is not part of the reachable scene path.

Depth-of-field and sharpness feedback are instructional approximations. They are intended to show the effect and direction of camera movements, not to provide metrological predictions for a physical camera and lens.
